Welcome to Weehawken, NJ!!!

Weehawken, NJ is a small town located in Hudson County, just across the Hudson River from Manhattan. Its close proximity to New York City makes it a convenient location for both residents and visitors. Weehawken is particularly advantageous for those who want to be near Times Square, as it is only about 3 miles away from the heart of the theater district. Visitors can easily access Times Square by taking public transportation, such as the New Jersey Transit bus or ferry, which run regularly throughout the day. Additionally, Weehawken offers stunning views of the Manhattan skyline, making it a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.
